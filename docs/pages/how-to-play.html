How to Play Huge Buildup

Short overview for hosting, joining and surviving each match.

Gameplay

Rules

  • Catchers convert runners by touching them.
  • A caught runner becomes catcher, the previous catcher becomes runner.
  • New catchers are frozen for a short time and cannot chain-catch instantly.
  • Power-ups spawn in the arena and activate instantly on pickup.

Controls

Default Keys

  • W A S D: Move
  • Mouse: Look around
  • Space: Jump
  • Esc: Free mouse / pause focus

Power-ups

Power-ups and Effects

Invisibility

Makes your character invisible for 6 seconds to other players.

Speed Boost

Increases movement speed for 5 seconds for quick chase or escape.

Teleport

Teleports you instantly to a random valid position in the arena.
